AI Crypto Trading and Training by TT

A free paper-trading trainer for 15 major cryptocurrencies. Live prices refresh every 60 seconds; each asset is scored 0–100 on four axes and given a letter grade (A+ to F) and a BUY / HOLD / SELL signal.

You trade with virtual money. There is no real money, no exchange account and no automated trading — every trade in the portfolio is one you place yourself. It is a practice tool, not investment advice.

The four rating axes

  • Momentum & trend — RSI, MACD and moving-average structure across timeframes
  • Volatility & risk — ATR, realised volatility, drawdown and risk-adjusted return
  • Market structure — market capitalisation, liquidity turnover, volume trend and spread
  • Relative strength — performance against Bitcoin and against the rest of the basket

Assets tracked

Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), BNB, XRP, Chainlink (LINK), Sui (SUI), Avalanche (AVAX), TRON (TRX), Cardano (ADA), Arbitrum (ARB), Ondo Finance (ONDO), Bittensor (TAO), Hyperliquid (HYPE) and Dogecoin (DOGE).

Live ratings for all 15 assets are available as JSON at /api/overview; service status is at /api/health.
